Writing a Strategy Guide

Subway surfers is an adventure game and fun to play.While some people play this game for refreshment purposes ,some people like me are completely into becoming the greatest subway surfers.In this short strategy guide ,I will provide you with some strategies and tricks that will help you master the game.

There are 4 major power-ups to enhance a specific ability of the character.

The following table identifies the power-up,capability and the way of activation.

POWERUP NAME CAPABILITY WAY OF ACTIVATION
Jetpack allows the character to fly in the sky gather it during running
2x Multiplier doubles the multiplier while running gather it during running
Magnet attract the neighbouring coins gather it during running
Super Sneakers makes the character jump high gather it during running

Practice the following tricks while playing:

  • stay on high ground
  • keep your keys safe
  • practice as much as you can
  • update your boosters frequently
  • swipe down to cancel your jump
  • activate your hoverboards whenever possible
  • atract the coins with super sneakers
  • plan your route

The toughest part in the game is to safeguard your character from the train inspector who appears when the character bumps into any hurdle.Always keep your character on high ground or on the trains so that it does not dodge into the obstacles on the ground.

The inspector has following features:

  • middle aged
  • overweight
  • has a brown moustache
  • followed by his dog always

Follow the ideas listed below to enhance your play:

  • Use a hoverboard that lasts for 30 seconds and allows the character to avoid any collision.
  • Complete Daily Challenges and Weekly Hunts to get rewards like free keys,coins and power boosters.
  • Try to collect the powerups as much as you can while running.
